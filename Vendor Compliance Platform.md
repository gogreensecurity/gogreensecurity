Real-Time Facility Access Control

GoGreen Security enables healthcare facilities to track vendors, contractors, and visitors in real time, ensuring full visibility and audit-ready reporting. With secure encrypted infrastructure, hospitals can maintain compliance while improving operational efficiency.
Smart Vendor Credentialing System
The platform simplifies vendor credentialing management by allowing hospitals to digitally collect, store, and monitor credentials. Features like background screening, expiration alerts, and centralized dashboards help ensure regulatory compliance at all times.
Mobile Audits & RFID Security
With mobile spot checks and RFID-enabled badges, staff can instantly verify credentials, conduct audits, and secure restricted areas. This reduces manual work while improving accuracy and response time.
Enhanced Operating Room Compliance
GoGreen Security’s Scrubs Kiosk Program ensures only verified personnel enter sensitive areas like operating rooms, supporting patient safety and uninterrupted workflows.

By combining real-time tracking, mobile technology, and compliance automation, GoGreen Security empowers healthcare facilities to operate securely and efficiently.
